Conference Participation

Realize LIVE 2025 – Siemens, Accenture, Stellantis

Attended Realize LIVE 2025 where the focus was on Software-Defined Vehicles (SDVs). Learned about the shift in the automotive industry toward centralized compute, domain fusion, and decoupling software from hardware. Key takeaway was the critical role of simulation, digital twins, and virtual sensors in accelerating AV development and validation.

AutoTech 2025 – Novi, Michigan

Explored cutting-edge developments in Connected Mobility, Virtual ECUs, and Vehicle Intelligence. The highlight was understanding how software is reshaping automotive architectures and enabling Over-the-Air updates beyond infotainment, affecting core vehicle control systems.

AutoTech Detroit 2024

Observed new vehicle technologies and participated in cybersecurity demonstrations. Built and tested an automotive cybersecurity testbench using 10 ECUs to analyze the effect of Denial-of-Service (DoS) attacks on vehicle systems. This hands-on experience deepened my understanding of in-vehicle network vulnerabilities and mitigation strategies.
