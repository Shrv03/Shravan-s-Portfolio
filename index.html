About Me

A highly self-motivated, detail-oriented, and forward-thinking Electrical Engineer with a strong passion for advancing next-generation automotive and robotic technologies. I am deeply driven by innovation in autonomous vehicles, ADAS, software-defined vehicles (SDVs), electric vehicles (EVs), and intelligent robotics. Through rigorous research and hands-on, solution-driven projects, I have developed expertise in V2X communication, Battery Management Systems (BMS), Electronic Control Units (ECUs), PCB design, and AI-powered perception and control systems. I am especially passionate about the integration of autonomous mobile robots (AMRs) within Industry 4.0 frameworks, enabling smarter, safer, and more efficient mobility and manufacturing. I bring a creative mindset, analytical problem-solving skills, and a relentless curiosity to every challenge in the evolving automotive and robotics landscape.
